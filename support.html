Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a new lead sheet?

Tap the "New Lead Sheet" button on the home screen. Fill in the customer information at the top, then scroll through the product list to enter quantities and prices for items the customer wants to order.

Where is my data stored?

All data is stored locally on your device. Your lead sheets never leave your device unless you explicitly export and share them.

How do I export a lead sheet?

From the home screen, tap the checkbox icon to enter selection mode. Select the lead sheets you want to export, then tap the "Export" button to generate a PDF that you can share via email, AirDrop, or any other sharing method.

Can I delete a lead sheet?

Yes. Enter selection mode by tapping the checkbox icon, select the lead sheets you want to remove, then tap the "Delete" button. You can also swipe left on a lead sheet to delete it.

Is my data backed up?

Lead sheets are stored in your device's local storage. We recommend regularly exporting important lead sheets as PDFs to ensure you have backups. If you uninstall the app, your data will be deleted.
